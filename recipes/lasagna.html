Recipe for Lasagna

Tasty Lasagna

Basic lasagna recipe for beginners!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg(s)

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
